Who We Are

The Novaversity Club, established on 13th March 2024, is one of the five prestigious clubs in the Department of CSE-Data Science at PSCMRCET. It was founded to create a platform that bridges academic knowledge with industry experience. Key Highlights: Offers internships, certification courses, and serves as a Center of Excellence. Fosters collaborations with top companies and industry experts. Focuses on professional skill development through hands-on projects and learning opportunities.
